Step 1: Be In Choice

Do You Have To Or Do You Choose To?

If you feel you have to do it, you are less likely to be successful at sustaining a new practice. Success comes with the internal accountability of committing to and owning it.

Step 4: The Learning Zone

Get Outside Your Comfort Zone and Into the Learning Zone.

Design your practice so that it is not so difficult that you will get discouraged and give up, but also not so easy that you will get bored and complacent.
